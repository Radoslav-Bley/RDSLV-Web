Privacy Policy.

Last updated: 12/05/2024

This Privacy Policy describes how Radoslav Bley ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, and shares personal data collected from visitors ("you", "your") to the website rdslv.sk ("Website").

1. Collection and Use of Personal Data

We collect personal data through the use of Google Search Console and Google Analytics for analytics purposes. The types of data collected may include:

  • IP addresses
  • Device information
  • Browser information
  • Demographic information
  • Website usage data

We use the personal data collected for analytics purposes to understand how visitors interact with our Website, improve our services, and optimize user experience.

2. Legal Basis for Processing

The legal basis for processing personal data for analytics purposes is your consent. By using our Website, you consent to the processing of your personal data in accordance with this Privacy Policy and our Cookie Policy.

3. Data Sharing and Transfers

We do not personally collect or share personal data collected through Google Search Console and Google Analytics. Data sharing and transfers are subject to the terms and policies of Google.

4. User Rights

As we do not directly collect or process personal data, your rights regarding personal data collected through Google Search Console and Google Analytics are subject to Google's policies and procedures. You may refer to Google's Privacy Policy and Google Analytics Terms of Service for more information.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

Our Website uses cookies and tracking technologies for analytics purposes. By using our Website, you consent to the use of cookies as described in our Cookie Policy.

6. Contact Information

If you choose to contact us via email or phone to inquire about our services, you may voluntarily provide us with personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, and any other information relevant to your inquiry. We will use this information solely for the purpose of responding to your inquiry and providing you with the requested services.

7. Security Measures

We take reasonable measures to protect personal data collected through Google Search Console and Google Analytics from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction.

8. Data Retention

We retain personal data collected through Google Search Console and Google Analytics for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes for which it was collected, unless a longer retention period is required or permitted by law.

9. Third-Party Services

Our Website may contain links to third-party websites or services that are not operated or controlled by us. This Privacy Policy does not apply to third-party websites or services. We recommend reviewing the privacy policies of these third parties before providing any personal data.

10. Children's Privacy

Our Website is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children under the age of 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe that your child has provided personal data on our Website, please contact us to have the data removed.

11. Changes to the Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The latest version of the Privacy Policy will be posted on the Website. Your continued use of the Website constitutes acceptance of the updated Privacy Policy.
